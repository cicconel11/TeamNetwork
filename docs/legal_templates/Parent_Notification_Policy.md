# Parent Notification: How TeamNetwork Protects Your Child's Information

*A plain-language guide for parents and guardians*

---

## Who is TeamNetwork?

TeamNetwork is a software service used by schools to manage student organizations such as athletic teams, clubs, and alumni networks. When your child joins a school organization that uses TeamNetwork, some basic information is collected to set up their membership.

This document explains what information is collected, how it is protected, and what rights you have.

---

## What Information Do We Collect?

TeamNetwork collects only the minimum information needed to manage organization membership:

| Information | Why It's Needed |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **First Name and Last Name** | To identify your child within the organization |
| **Email Address** | To send important updates and allow your child to log in |
| **Graduation Year** | To group students by class year and manage alumni status |
| **Profile Photo** (optional) | To help organization members recognize each other |

---

## What We DO NOT Collect

TeamNetwork does **not** collect sensitive educational or personal records. The following information is never stored in our system:

- Social Security Numbers
- Student ID Numbers
- Date of Birth
- Home Address
- Grades or GPA
- Transcripts
- Attendance Records
- Disciplinary Records
- Health or Medical Records

Your child's academic performance and personal records remain solely with the school.

---

## How Is My Child's Information Protected?

We take data security seriously and use multiple layers of protection:

### Encryption
- All data is **encrypted** when stored in our database
- All data is **encrypted** when transmitted over the internet
- We use the same encryption standards as major banks

### Access Controls
- Only organization administrators (coaches, advisors) can view member information
- Students can only see information about members of their own organizations
- Technical systems prevent unauthorized access at every level

### No Advertising or Data Sales
- We **never** sell student information to anyone
- We **never** use student data for advertising
- We **never** share information with marketers

---

## Who Can See My Child's Information?

Access to your child's information is strictly limited:

| Who | What They Can See |
|-----|-------------------|
| **Organization Administrators** (coaches, advisors) | All member information for their organization |
| **Other Organization Members** | Name and profile photo of fellow members |
| **School Officials** | Member information for organizations at their school |
| **TeamNetwork Technical Staff** | Access only for technical support, with security controls |

No one outside these groups can access your child's information.

---

## Your Rights as a Parent

Under federal law (FERPA), you have the right to:

### Review Your Child's Information
You may request to see what information TeamNetwork stores about your child. Contact your school administrator to make this request.

### Request Corrections
If any information is inaccurate, you may request corrections through your school administrator.

### Request Deletion
You may request that your child's information be removed from TeamNetwork. Contact your school administrator to initiate this process.

### Ask Questions
If you have concerns about how your child's data is handled, contact your school administrator. They can provide additional information or escalate concerns to TeamNetwork.

---

## How Long Is Data Kept?

Your child's information is kept only as long as they are an active member of a school organization using TeamNetwork. When your child graduates or leaves the organization:

- Their account can be converted to alumni status (with limited information retained)
- Their account can be deleted entirely upon request
- If the school stops using TeamNetwork, all student data is deleted within 60 days
