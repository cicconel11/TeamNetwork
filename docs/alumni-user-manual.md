# Alumni User Manual

Welcome to TeamNetwork! Here's everything you need to get started.

---

## Where to Find Everything

| I want to... | Go to |
|--------------|-------|
| See what's happening | **Home** (your org's main page) |
| Find alumni | **Alumni** in sidebar |
| Browse current members | **Members** in sidebar |
| See/RSVP to events | **Calendar** in sidebar |
| Find or post jobs | **Jobs** in sidebar |
| Become a mentor | **Mentorship** in sidebar |
| Join discussions | **Messages** in sidebar |
| Make a donation | **Philanthropy** in sidebar |
| Edit my profile | Click your avatar → **My Profile** → **Edit** |
| Connect LinkedIn | **Settings** → **Connected Accounts** |
| Sync my calendar | **Calendar** → **My Settings** |

---

## 1. Set Up Your Profile

**Where:** Click your avatar → **My Profile** → **Edit**

Fill in:
- Photo, name, job title, company
- City, school, major, graduation year
- Bio and LinkedIn URL

Click **Save** when done.

---

## 2. Connect LinkedIn

**Where:** **Settings** → **Connected Accounts**

**Step 1: Connect your account**
1. Click **Connect LinkedIn**
2. Approve on LinkedIn
3. Done — your name, email, and photo are imported

**Step 2: Add your LinkedIn URL**
1. Enter your URL: `https://linkedin.com/in/yourname`
2. Click **Save**
3. Click **Sync Data** to import your work history and education

You can sync up to 2 times per month. Make sure your LinkedIn profile sections are set to public.

---

## 3. Find Alumni

**Where:** **Alumni** in sidebar

Use filters to narrow your search:
- Graduation Year
- Industry
- Company
- City
- Position

Click any alumni card to see their full profile. Contact them via email or LinkedIn. Phone may be available if shared.

---

## 4. Become a Mentor

**Where:** **Mentorship** in sidebar

1. Click **Become a Mentor**
2. Fill in your bio, expertise areas, and contact info
3. Submit

Once registered, you'll appear in the mentor directory. Admins may pair you with mentees, or you can select your own from available members. Log sessions to track progress.

---

## 5. Jobs

**Where:** **Jobs** in sidebar

**Find jobs:** Browse listings and filter by location type, experience level, industry, or company.

**Post a job:**
1. Click **Post Job**
2. Fill in title, company, description, and details
3. Submit

You can edit or delete your postings anytime.

---

## 6. Events

**Where:** **Calendar** in sidebar

- View events in month or list view
- Click any event for details
- Click **Attending**, **Maybe**, or **Not Attending** to RSVP

**Sync to your calendar:**
1. Go to **Calendar** → **My Settings**
2. Connect Google or Outlook
3. Choose which event types to sync

---

## 7. Community Feed

**Where:** **Home** (your org's main page)

**Post:** Click "What's on your mind?" → write your post → add images or a poll → click **Post**

**Engage:** Like posts (heart icon), comment, or vote in polls.

---

## 8. Discussions

**Where:** **Messages** in sidebar

**Start a discussion:** Click **New Discussion** → add title and description → submit

**Reply:** Click any thread → type your reply → click **Send** or press Enter

---

## 9. Make a Donation

**Where:** **Philanthropy** in sidebar

1. Enter amount and optional name/email
2. Choose designation (general, specific campaign, or custom)
3. Complete captcha
4. Click **Donate** and pay via Stripe

---

## Privacy Note

Your profile information (name, photo, job, education) is visible to other members of your organization. Contact details like email are shown on your profile; phone number is only visible if you choose to add it. You control what you share when editing your profile.

---

## Quick Tips

- Complete your profile to be more discoverable
- Connect LinkedIn early to auto-fill your work history
- Register as a mentor to give back
- Post job opportunities to help fellow alumni
- RSVP to events to stay connected
- Sync your calendar so you never miss anything
